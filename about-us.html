About US

Founded in 2023 by Chandrakanth Avusali and Kiran Kumar Nalla, Payfikar commenced operations as a mobile wallet to make digital payments convenient for our users. Payfikar is providing payment solutions platform for Mobile Recharge, DTH and Utility bill payment and offering attractive Cash-back against the services we provide. Digitally paying users have been rapidly growing in India in the last decade, but the credit card penetration in the country is still only 3.5%. We are focused on addressing the unmet credit needs of the fast-growing digitally paying users by combining the convenience of everyday mobile payments with the benefits of Cashback, Coupons & Reward points. In a very short span of time, we have experienced rapid growth and have established our company as one of the leading players in the Indian market.
